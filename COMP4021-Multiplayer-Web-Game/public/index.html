Arcane Alpha

Welcome back to the Trio Wizard Tournament, aspiring champions!

In the following task, you will be form groups of three, use the spells you know, and defeat your enemies.

The last group standing shall be crowned champion and enter the next round!

Controls

W/A/S/D: Move

E: Interact

LMB: Primary Spell

RMB: Secondary Spell

SPELLS

A non-charged wand will be given to each participant.

You will be unable to cast a spell with the wand until you have find the respective rune of the spell.

SHROUD

A magical shroud will close in soon after the match starts. Within minutes, the whole arena will be covered in deadly mist.

Outrun the shroud or perish in the dark!

CHEAT MODE

Extra powerful dark wizards in the past have been said to cheat with a forbidden technique by pressing the Delete Button.

Please make sure not to accidentally press your Delete Button during the match or you may accidentally cast the killing curse.
